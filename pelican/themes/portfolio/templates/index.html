About Me

Professionally

I'm a Junior Software Developer who enjoys working on anything that makes sense and has real value. I'm not afraid of new challenges and I like learning new technologies and approaches. I've been involved in computer science for years, during which I've built a technical foundation - both through studying at the Faculty of Information Technology at BUT in Brno and creating my own projects, including team collaboration.

Personally

I'm a tech enthusiast through and through - I've always enjoyed creating new things and improving them. Whether it was drawings, Lego models, video games, or later software, I enjoy creating and finding ways to improve what I do. Since elementary school, I knew I wanted to combine technology and creativity - that's why I decided to pursue IT.

When creating (whether SW projects or amateur woodworking), I believe in the importance of good planning and design. I think the question "why?" or "what for?" is as important as "how?".
